# Confidence and Risk Model

Frameward balances progress with clarification.

## Confidence score

- 0.8–1.0: proceed
- 0.5–0.79: proceed with clear assumptions
- 0.3–0.49: ask one simple question unless low-risk
- below 0.3: ask a clarifying question before editing

## Risk score

Risk increases when a change affects:

- checkout
- signup
- authentication
- payments
- destructive actions
- regulated data
- accessibility-critical flows
- navigation architecture

## Rule

High-risk and low-confidence tasks require clarification. Low-risk and medium-confidence tasks should proceed with assumptions.
